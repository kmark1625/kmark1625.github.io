HTML5 Cheatsheet

Element: Header

Why should you use: Adds semantic value to the section's heading and related content.

When to use: Use at the beginning of your page or at the start of a section.

Tips:

  • You can use multiple headers in a document. Each header element will become the header for that section of your document.
  • You are required to have at least one heading tag (h1-h6) inside of this element or the nav element.

Element: Footer

Why should you use: Adds semantic value to the footer of a document or section.

When to use: Place at the bottom of your page or at the bottom of a section

Tips:

  • Usually contains author information, copyright information, contact information, or related documents.
  • Contact information should be placed inside an address element.

Element: Article

Why should you use: This should be used to segment independent pieces of content.

When to use: This should be used for a self-contained composition.

Tips:

  • Article provides more semantic meaning than section.
  • Article should be used if its content can stand alone.
